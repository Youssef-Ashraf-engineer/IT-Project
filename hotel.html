Guest Help Center

🛎️ Booking & Reservations

Guests can book rooms through the website, mobile app, or directly at the hotel reception. After booking, you will receive a confirmation email with all reservation details.

In most cases, cancellations are free if done at least 24–48 hours before check-in. However, non-refundable deals offer lower prices but cannot be changed or canceled.

🍽️ Meals & Dining

Breakfast is served daily in buffet style, offering both local Egyptian dishes and international options. It is included in selected room packages, while other bookings may require an extra fee.

The hotel also features a rooftop restaurant, café lounge, and 24-hour room service. Vegetarian and special diet meals can be requested in advance.

🕒 Check-in & Check-out

Standard check-in starts at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in depends on room availability and may include additional charges.

Late check-out is sometimes available for loyal guests or direct bookings, but must be requested in advance.

📶 Facilities & Services

Guests have access to free high-speed WiFi across the entire property, including rooms and public areas. The hotel also includes a swimming pool, fitness center, spa, and business meeting rooms.

Additional services include airport transfers, laundry service, and guided tour booking assistance.

🚫 Policies & Rules

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside rooms but allowed in designated outdoor areas. Pets are not allowed except for certified service animals.

Guests must respect quiet hours between 11:00 PM and 7:00 AM to ensure comfort for all visitors. Violating policies may result in additional charges or booking cancellation.
